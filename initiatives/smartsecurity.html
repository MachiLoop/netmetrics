ICT4ALL "THE SMART TRAINING"

Project Overview

ICT4ALL "THE SMART TRAINING" ICT4ALL "THE SMART TRAINING" is a comprehensive ICT security and cyber security program aimed at producing 100,000 professionals in Nigeria who can effectively integrate and leverage computer security and its applications. The program emphasizes practicality, economy, safety, standards, comfort, and aesthetics in implementing ICT security measures.

Partnership with Nigeria Security and Civil Defense Corps:

ICT4ALL "THE SMART TRAINING" program involves a partnership and engagement with the Nigeria Security and Civil Defense Corps (NSCDC) to build effective smart security solutions. This collaboration aims to enhance the overall security infrastructure in Nigeria by leveraging ICT tools and techniques.

Services Offered

Mobile Tracking Training:Training individuals in mobile tracking techniques to enhance security and track mobile devices in case of theft or loss.

Cybersecurity: Providing training and expertise in cybersecurity, focusing on protecting computer systems, networks, and data from cyber threats.

Kidnappers Tracker: Developing and implementing technologies and techniques to track and combat kidnapping incidents. Cryptocurrency Fraud Investigations: Training professionals in investigating fraud related to cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology.

Mobile Forensics: Equipping individuals with the skills and knowledge to conduct forensic investigations on mobile devices, recovering evidence for legal proceedings.

CCTV Installation and Investigation: Offering services related to the installation and operation of closed-circuit television (CCTV) systems for surveillance and investigation purposes.

Cyber and Digital Forensics: Providing expertise in investigating cybercrimes, collecting digital evidence, and analyzing digital data.

Financial Crime Investigation: Training professionals in investigating financial crimes such as fraud, money laundering, and identity theft.

Fraud Investigation: Conducting investigations into various types of fraud, including online scams, identity theft, and financial fraud.

Anti-Terrorism Mapping: Utilizing ICT tools to create maps and analyze data for anti-terrorism efforts, enhancing security and counter-terrorism strategies.

Investigative Journalism: Training journalists in utilizing ICT tools for investigative reporting, focusing on exposing corruption and societal issues.

Boot Structure

Similar to the previous project mentioned, ICT4ALL "THE SMART TRAINING" program operates under the BOOT (Build, Own, Operate, and Transfer) structure. This means that Netmetric initially builds the required infrastructure and provides the necessary resources, training, and services. Netmetric also owns and operates the program during a specified period. Eventually, the ownership and operation of the program will be transferred to the relevant stakeholders, ensuring long-term sustainability and continuity.

In summary, ICT4ALL "THE SMART TRAINING" program is a comprehensive ICT security and cyber security initiative aimed at producing a large number of professionals in Nigeria. Through partnerships, engagement with security agencies, and a range of services, the program addresses various aspects of ICT security and contributes to the overall safety and security landscape in Nigeria. The BOOT structure ensures the program's sustainability and long-term impact.
