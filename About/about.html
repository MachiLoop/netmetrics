About Us

Who are We?

Netmetric Indian Of Information Technology Limited is a globally recognized IT education and training company. We are registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) under the RC: 1207813. Our core business focuses on providing Information Technology (IT) services, consulting, human capital development, and capacity building. Since the late 2012s, we have made significant investments in ICT infrastructure in schools and have provided ICT training for teachers and other professionals. However, there has been limited national research evidence on the impact of this new technology on teaching and learning in schools. We take pride in being the initiator of the ICT4ALL Solution, which is dedicated to empowering children through ICT and capacity building. As a lead consulting partner, we collaborate with international, local, corporate, ICT organizations, institutions, associations, and societies to develop ICT education and manage innovations. As a socially responsible firm, we strongly believe that the social impact resulting from problem-solving is a major measure of business success. That's why we actively create solutions to social and economic problems within our immediate society through public and private partnership projects (PPP). At Netmetric, our primary focus is to analyze the contemporary Nigerian society, identify problematic areas across all levels of the economy, and propose holistic solutions based on ICT development and appropriate software solutions. Our guiding principle is "what is new and what is next" to effectively address present and future challenges in the field of ICT

Our Mission

our mission at Netmetric is to ensure that technology management stays up to date with the current and future challenges related to managing information assets in an era encompassing social media, mobile technology, cloud computing, big data, cyber security, and innovation management. We are dedicated to developing and maintaining a premium information management system that effectively addresses these challenges and enables organizations to thrive in the digital age.

Our Vision

our vision is centered around developing human capacity through international ICT empowerment programs. We aspire to be a driving force in shaping information management trends and educational requirements, ultimately creating a pool of competent professionals ready to tackle the challenges of the digital era.
